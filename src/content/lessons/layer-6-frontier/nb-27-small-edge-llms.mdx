## What this lesson is really about

Edge models force clarity. You cannot hide behind unlimited context, cloud elasticity, or huge memory budgets. Every choice becomes more explicit.

Smaller models matter because many products need low latency, lower cost, privacy-preserving behavior, or local execution paths that cloud-first designs cannot offer.

## What you will understand

- Reason about when smaller models are the better product decision.
- Understand hardware and memory constraints at the edge.
- Compare quantization and compression choices with more nuance.
- Think about privacy, responsiveness, and offline use as product features.

## Why this topic matters in real AI engineering

Study small and edge-deployable LLMs through compression, hardware limits, quantization, and user experience tradeoffs. This lesson should help you notice which parts are conceptual, which parts are operational, and which parts are the usual source of failure when a team tries to move too quickly.

## How to use this lesson

Read the sections in order the first time. On later passes, use the subtopic map below as a reference spine: it is arranged to help you connect ideas rather than memorize isolated terms.

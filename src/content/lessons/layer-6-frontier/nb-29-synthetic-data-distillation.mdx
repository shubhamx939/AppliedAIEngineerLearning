## What this lesson is really about

Synthetic data and distillation are attractive because they promise leverage. The real question is what exactly is being transferred, compressed, or potentially corrupted in the process.

This lesson matters because teams often use these methods to reduce cost or bootstrap capability, but the quality control challenge is easy to underestimate.

## What you will understand

- Explain what synthetic data can and cannot substitute for.
- Understand distillation as a transfer of behavior under constraints.
- Recognize quality risks in generated supervision signals.
- Use these methods more strategically in data-scarce settings.

## Why this topic matters in real AI engineering

Understand synthetic data and distillation as ways to shift capability, cost, and supervision strategy rather than as magic shortcuts. This lesson should help you notice which parts are conceptual, which parts are operational, and which parts are the usual source of failure when a team tries to move too quickly.

## How to use this lesson

Read the sections in order the first time. On later passes, use the subtopic map below as a reference spine: it is arranged to help you connect ideas rather than memorize isolated terms.

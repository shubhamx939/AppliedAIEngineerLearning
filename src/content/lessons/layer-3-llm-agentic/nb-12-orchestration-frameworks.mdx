## What this lesson is really about

Frameworks become useful when you understand the problem they are compressing. Without that, it is easy to inherit complexity that the product never needed.

Senior engineers evaluate these libraries by their state model, error handling, and operational visibility, not by surface-level demos.

## What you will understand

- Understand what orchestration frameworks are trying to abstract.
- Compare framework choices based on debugging, flexibility, and production fit.
- Avoid overfitting architecture to a library trend.
- Design simpler execution graphs before reaching for a framework.

## Why this topic matters in real AI engineering

Compare agent orchestration frameworks through the lens of state, execution model, ergonomics, and operational clarity. This lesson should help you notice which parts are conceptual, which parts are operational, and which parts are the usual source of failure when a team tries to move too quickly.

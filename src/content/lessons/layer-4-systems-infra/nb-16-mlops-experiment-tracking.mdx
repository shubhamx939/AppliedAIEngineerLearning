## What this lesson is really about

Experiment tracking sounds administrative until you lose the ability to explain why a model improved, regressed, or cannot be reproduced. Then it becomes essential engineering hygiene.

MLOps is valuable when it reduces ambiguity. This lesson focuses on that core purpose rather than on tool worship.

## What you will understand

- Track model and experiment state without relying on memory.
- Understand lineage across datasets, parameters, and artifacts.
- Reduce confusion between exploration and productionized training.
- Design workflows that make comparison and rollback possible.

## Why this topic matters in real AI engineering

Learn the MLOps layer that keeps experiments understandable: tracking, reproducibility, lineage, and controlled iteration. This lesson should help you notice which parts are conceptual, which parts are operational, and which parts are the usual source of failure when a team tries to move too quickly.

## How to use this lesson

Read the sections in order the first time. On later passes, use the subtopic map below as a reference spine: it is arranged to help you connect ideas rather than memorize isolated terms.

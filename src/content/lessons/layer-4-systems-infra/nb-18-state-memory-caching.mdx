## What this lesson is really about

State and memory are often discussed as if they were the same thing. In practice, they solve different problems: continuity, speed, personalization, and cost control.

This distinction matters a lot in AI applications, where context can be expensive and user trust is sensitive to stale or over-personalized behavior.

## What you will understand

- Separate transient session state from reusable memory and cached artifacts.
- Choose the right storage scope for different AI interactions.
- Understand freshness, invalidation, and privacy tradeoffs.
- Design continuity features without creating hidden system confusion.

## Why this topic matters in real AI engineering

Understand state, memory, and caching as different tools for preserving continuity in AI systems. This lesson should help you notice which parts are conceptual, which parts are operational, and which parts are the usual source of failure when a team tries to move too quickly.
